Lesson 1

Kitchen Safety Essentials

Small habits prevent the biggest mistakes. Master these before you start cooking.

Always Remember

  • Wash hands before and after handling ingredients.
  • Use separate cutting boards for raw meat and vegetables.
  • Keep counters and utensils clean while cooking.
  • Check internal food temperature before serving.
